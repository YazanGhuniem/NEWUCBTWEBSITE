About

UC Better Tomorrow is a student-led organization focused on supporting Cincinnati's homeless community through direct action and compassion. From cooking meals at local shelters to organizing donation drives and outreach events, we believe small efforts can lead to big change. Our mission is to create a consistent, hands-on impact while fostering empathy and community among students.
